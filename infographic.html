INFOGRAPHIC

Design and infographic using Adobe Indesign that properly displays a student-chosen technology's role in the communication system and it's impacts.

Infographic Project

A USB flash drive is a compact storage unit that uses built-in USB ports on computers and other devices to quickly transfer file information from one device to another.

This infographic was designed based on three different factors, color cohesion, readability, and simplicity. A USB flash drive was chosen due to it's simple design and straightforward usage. The first step when creating the overall design of the infographic consisted of sketching an outline the required information and citations. Once all elements of the infographic were planned on paper, it was time to move into Adobe Indesign to finalize the colors, graphics, fonts, and other design details.

After finalizing the infographic, it was converted to a PDF for electronic submission and printed in full color for class viewing.

Resources Used: How Does A Flash Drive Work?, How The Simple USB Has Changed Computing, Wikipedia
