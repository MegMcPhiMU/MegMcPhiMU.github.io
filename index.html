Introduction and description of website.

Student and CADD Lab Assistant at Millersville University

Megan McPhillips is a junior Millersville University student working towards a bachelor's degree in Applied Engineering and Technology Management with a concentration in Computer-Aided Drafting and Design, as well as two minors, one in Biology and one in Occupational Health and Safety. She plans on continuing her education after college towards a master's in bioengineering, and potentially a PHd if money and time permits. In the meantime, she hopes to enter either the construction or mechanical engineering fields as a CADD designer.

This website will be used to quickly and conveniently display various different projects and assignments submitted by Megan during the fall session of AENG 110.01: Communication and Information Systems in 2023.
